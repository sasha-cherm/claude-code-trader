### 2. CRITICAL LEARNING: get_price() vs actual fill price
- `get_price(tid, 'buy')` returns the **BID** (what others pay). NOT the fill price.
- Actual fills for political markets were MUCH higher than bid:
  - Margin 5-10%: bid=0.31, ask=0.41, **actual fill=0.54**
  - LA 3rd: bid=0.42, ask=0.47, **actual fill=0.47**
- For future trades: use the **sell price (ask)** or higher as expected fill, not the buy (bid).
- This means edge estimates from the bid price are inflated. Always verify expected fill vs true probability.
