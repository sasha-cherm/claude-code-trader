### Critical Discovery: CLOB Market Orders Work, Limit Orders Don't
- **The CLOB resting orderbook is empty** for all sports markets — bids at 0.01, asks at 0.99
- **Limit orders placed at reasonable prices don't fill** — sit unfilled in the book
- **Market orders (MarketOrderArgs) fill at fair value** through dynamic market makers
- The `get_price(token, 'buy'/'sell')` method returns accurate execution prices
- This is the critical mechanism for trading: use MarketOrderArgs, NOT OrderArgs
